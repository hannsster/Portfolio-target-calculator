Overview
A lightweight, privacy-focused rebalancing tool designed to help you calculate exact share purchases for your portfolio. This tool does not require your login credentials and keeps all data locally on your machine.

How to Use

Setup Your Portfolio: Use the search bar to add your tickers.

Set Targets: Enter your "Target %" for each asset. Ensure the total of all targets equals 100%.

Log Holdings: Enter your current holdings in either Dollars or Shares using the toggle at the top.

Plan Your Trade:

Enter the amount of cash you are about to deposit/invest.

Click "Generate Execution Plan."

The app will fetch live prices and tell you exactly how to spend your deposit to get as close to your targets as possible.

Execute: Once you’ve made the trades in your broker of choice, click "Apply to Portfolio" to update your local totals.

Key Features

Dynamic Backgrounds: Click the "Settings" (folder) icon to select a folder of images. The app will pull colors from your wallpapers to theme the UI.

Privacy First: Your data never leaves your computer. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

Smart Math: The tool uses proportional allocation, meaning it prioritizes the assets furthest from their targets based only on the cash you have available.

Troubleshooting

Price Sync: If prices look outdated, clicking "Generate Execution Plan" forces a fresh sync with market data.

Search Issues: The search requires a minimum of 2 characters to trigger.
