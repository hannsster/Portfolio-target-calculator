# Portfolio Rebalancer Pro

A lightweight, privacy-focused rebalancing tool designed to help you calculate exact share purchases for your portfolio. This tool **does not require login credentials** and keeps all data locally on your machine.

---

## How to Use

1. **Setup Your Portfolio**: Use the search bar to add your tickers.
2. **Set Targets**: Enter your **Target %** for each asset. 
   > *Note: Ensure the total of all targets equals 100%.*
3. **Log Holdings**: Enter your current holdings in either **Dollars** or **Shares** using the toggle at the top.
4. **Plan Your Trade**:
   - Enter the amount of cash you are about to deposit/invest.
   - Click **"Generate Execution Plan."**
   - The app fetches live prices and calculates exactly how to spend your deposit to reach your targets.
5. **Execute**: Once you’ve made the trades in your broker, click **"Apply to Portfolio"** to update your local totals.

---

## Key Features

* **Dynamic Backgrounds**: Click the **Settings (folder) icon** to select a folder of images. The app pulls colors from your wallpapers to theme the UI automatically.
* **Privacy First**: Your data never leaves your computer. No accounts, no cloud, and no tracking.
* **Smart Math**: Uses proportional allocation to prioritize assets furthest from their targets based on your available cash.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Price Sync** | Click "Generate Execution Plan" to force a fresh sync with market data. |
| **Search Issues** | The search requires a minimum of **2 characters** to trigger. |
